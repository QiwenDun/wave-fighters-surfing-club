Club Services

The following is the main business in Wave Fighters, please check the details according to your needs.

  • Surf Lessons

    If you're interested in surfing but have never tried surfing, a beginner's course is the best first step. Here we will tailor your study plan to your situation, and have the opportunity to communicate with professional surfers




    See Details
  • Vocational Training

    We work closely with official surfing organisations, so if you love surfing and want to become a professional surfer, or decide to pursue a career in surfing in the future, you can join our vocational training courses




    See Details
  • Surfboard Shop

    We have the most complete surfboard store and gift shop, you can buy any surfboard you like according to your needs, it can be any color or brand. We also offer custom made surfboards for professionals.





    Go shop
  • Regular Activities

    Wave Fighters hold some events and competitions on a regular basis, we invite some heavyweight surfers and surf teams to compete. If you have your own surf team, or you are interested in these competitions and events, please don't hold back, you are welcome anytime Come and watch.

Testimonials from club members
The first time I surfed, I fell in love with this sport. It is a struggle between man and nature, and it is also a manifestation of skill. Only by standing on a surfboard can you appreciate its excitement and vitality. Surfing gives me a novel experience. Without hard work, how can success be achieved; without hardships, where can glory come from; without setbacks, where can glory come? Only by paying can you get what you deserve.

The more the waves approached me, the more passionate they became, and they were like a horizontal formation of countless beauties, rushing towards me at a fast-paced emotional rhythm. When I raised my head, revealing one after another, one after another with youthful smiles, and held up countless silver flowers to me...




Although the waves are loud, I feel calm when I surf, and I fell in love with surfing. Many of my tips are from this club, if you are looking for a professional and friendly club this is for you.
